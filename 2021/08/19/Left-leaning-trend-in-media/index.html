Left leaning trend in media

Posted on 2021-08-19

Topic：Does the media have liberal bias?
Ideas: the answer is yes
Several aspects.
1 ultimated reasons: explain the nowadays society and media will have the bias, why its is inevitable in the background situation
Examples: job occupations data opinions

Let me begin by defining three terms to begin with today’s topic: what is objective in media? It means that you report a news without own political views or instincts slanting the story to one and another. It’s pretty hard, isn’t it? We all had intentions to write about things. While nowadays mainstream media, especially for those which had international influence, tended to have literally biased in their opinions.

Let us take America as an example. We can count on the mainstream newspaper such as New York Times, The economics, CNN, LA Times, etc. But thinking of the right wing media, the most famous one would be The fox. From the above data, it seems that there are more left-wing newspapers than right-wing newspapers. There are several social contextual reasons why I say that the left-leaning of the American media is inevitable.

First, the trend of intellectual currents in modern American society is generally politically correct, which means that in practice people are limited in expressing their views. I’m not going to presume to talk about American politics here, but in such a social context, newspapers also tend to cater more to the views and tastes of the public. From a media science point of view, neutral coverage of a big event does not attract an audience - the event itself is dead, but people are alive. There are many different ways to cover the same event, and the fastest topping hot story must also carry strong emotions - resonating with the audience, making the reader feel strong emotions and thus identify with: the point of view, the author’s point of view, the view of the party represented.

I will give some examples to demonstrate this below. During President Bush’s party administration, the media had shamelessly glorified Cindy Sheehan. In creating story, she was charactized as a poor woman who wanted to see president Bush because her son passed away in the war of Iraq. However, she actually on the other side and in favor of the Iraqi. Well now you can see even in my speech there is bias. The mainstream media has the power to make the story big, and till now when you search on the chrome, Cindy Sheehan is treated as the anti-activist of America.

Also, for the passage of New York time which posted before the presidential election in Oct 2020, has posted about the achievements when Trump was on the stage. In the article, the author claimed that from 2016 to 2020, the number of racists risen, the criminals increased, but he failed to give any data to prove his idea——he gave out tondruas of opinions, of how bad was Trump, however we cannot let the emotions overwhelm our reason: are there anybody would admit themselves as a “racist?” if not, what events can defined as racialism, and how to do the statistics? Think about them and you will find loads of problem.

One editor of the Fox who worked there for more than 20 years told a story about his student, we will call him A here. A was graduated from a prominent university, and his educational attainment filled the requirement. While the interviewer asked him about his political background, he stated that he supported the right wing and pitied, he failed to get that job. I am not saying this ain’t correct, because a company has the right to select the people who shared same value with them, but still this evidence can prove that, we do things, write things, with our opinion, with our bias.
