Introduce of Literature Club

Posted on 2020-10-18

I select running my Literature Club as not only my CAS project but also my main out-school activity. I posited the club manager and my job is to organize where we hold each meeting every time as well as the content we discuss. Moreover, I set tasks on each occupation for my club members, so that they can finish their work in time.

Literature club is not just for entertainment after school, but also a place for everyone to have more or less progress on member’s language study in school, for everyone to freely express their ideas on a diversity of books, or even political affairs. Our aim is to provide somewhere students are able to share their thoughts without being criticized. Moreover, since I handed in the application form to school for a start investment, we have $900 to purchase books for members, of course, all of the magazines, books, readings are belong to Club.

Usually, I organize literature meeting once a week. We have different topics every time, based on the principle of democracy, before the talk there will be a vote in group chat for members to choose their favorite topic. We offer the selections in a variety of fields, including Ancient Chinese poets, Latin-America Literature, Europe and America history, Japanese culture, etc. Despite these, we emphasize on different methods on writing as well, for instance, a research on Stream of Conscious help us better understand why Virginia Woolf’s work impress on us that much, and why she could capture the echos brilliantly where a society lunch is apparently magicked into existence.

Moreover, my club had an official account on WeChat to show our articles after activities. There are some articles which written by myself. All of the members have the capacity and chance to contribute their own passages. Good poets as well as literature pieces of work are placed on our account.

Story of us ain’t over, and everyone takes the charge of continuing our dream on literature.
