My first MT

Posted on 2020-07-25

MT

So excited that we finally process the tournament on July, 19th. I have waited for the day for a long time. Our team was a new team, which means that many of us the first time(even though I am not the first time actually, but it’s have been half of a year I haven’t taken part in before).
On the first day, we met team 10. Our party representative was the defence. It was really awesome for their lay witness acting so dramatically. Honestly, they scared me bit because of being aggressive haha, although I aware that the tournament is “Mock trial”, not “who is more aggressive”. I adjust to the attitude in an hour and trying to deal with “trouble”. They were really nice people outside the competition.

Here is my cross examination below.
