preparing for a long time

Posted on 2020-07-11

There are only one week left to compete in NUMT this summer. In the tournament, I will be the expert witness from plaintiff, and the council of lay witness from defense. The case is very special, you can find only one expert in the whole competition(which means, no expert in the defense, hence this role is crucial.). during this period from the genning of July till now(11th), I have been preparing for a long time. Reciting all of my affidavit is hard, especially when you need to tell others your profession background preciously, otherwise the opposing council can object you.

I have finished my direct examination to Taylor(lay witness), and the following content is my DX.

Is the prosecution ready to its third witness?

A: Yes, your honor. The prosecution calls Mr. Strombom (the witness’s character name) to stand.

A (to W): Good morning, Mr.Strombom . Would you please state your name for the record.

W: Taylor Strombom, T A Y L O R, S T R O M B O M.

Section I

A: Would you like to briefly introduce yourself to members of the jury?

W: Fine. I am a sophomore at Midlands State University, as well as the Social Chair for Mu Tan, my co-ed Student Social Organization.

A: Can you describe your relationship with Sinclair?

W: Sure. He was my pledge parent when I was rushing MU Tan, but we don’t really know each other. Instead, he walks closer to Alex, to be more specific, he lives with him.

A: Do you know anything between Sinclair and Alex?

W: Only a bit. I think they use the lack of RA supervision to run their little business, but Skyler never explicitly mentioned it to me. I’ve heard that they were working for Malcolm, I just can’t believed that.

A: Do you know anything about Malcolm?

W: Only some rumors. I heard that Malcolm is the organized crime lord of Midlands. No one disobeys him lives and tell about it. This sounds terrible.

Section II

A: Do you know which course Sinclair took part in

W: In the spring of 2016, Skyler was enrolled in the Music in Midlands class. I cannot tell if Skyler attended every time, since there are about 60 students in class. But if I saw him, I always sit next to him.

A: Is Alex in your class as well?

W: Not exactly, but he’s in the intramural softball team. We all play for the Mu Tan team. Alex is a good player, taller than Skyler(about 3 inches), using an orange bat. He claimed that it brings him luck, and I have never seen anyone touch it.

A: Since Alex is such a good player, did your team win the game?

W: No. We all had a shot at winning because Alex is so excellent, but unfortunately, he fell onto the floor in the Rec Center and had a surgery.

Section III

Let’s move to the next part.

A: what happened to Alex after he repaired his leg？

W: In the March of 2016, it seemed like Alex and Skyler were arguing. I saw them at Mu Tan’s annual party.

A: What did they look like at the party?

W:Skyler was in all black, black ski mask and black flip-flops. Alex wore a cheap cop uniform that might be bought from Toby Supplies.
